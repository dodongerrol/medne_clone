HEALTH SCREENING

We provide one complementary basic health screening per subscription year.

You may upgrade to premuim health screening package at a lower rate exclusively just for you and your team.

OUTPATIENT GP

100% consultation cost will be covered under Mednefits network GP clinics.

You and your team only need to pay for medicine or any treatment done in the clinic.

DENTAL CARE

You and your team will receive up to 30% off selected basic dental services under mednefits network dental clinics. Services included such as scaling and polishing, tooth fillings, and gum treatments.

HEALTH SPECIALIST

You and your team will receive up to 60% off on specialist consultation under Mednefits network specialist. Services included such as opthalmology, orthopaedic, physiotherapy, and more.

TRADITIONAL CHINESE MEDICINE

100% consultation cost will be covered under Mednefits network TCM partners.

You and your team only need to pay for medicine or any treatment done in the clinic.
